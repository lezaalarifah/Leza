My Favourite Food is Indian Food

Indian Food is rich in flavour, full of spices, and very colourful.

Indian Food is my favourite food for the following reasons:-

  • I love meals that include basmati rice.
  • Spicy sauces, and tender chicken.
  • The smell of Indian spices makes me feel hungry immediately.

Another reasons

  1. I enjoy eating Indian food with friends and family because it brings people together and creates a warm atmosphere.
  2. I like Indian cuisine is that there are many choices, from mild buttery meals to very hot dishes for people who enjoy spicy food.
  3. I also like how Indian meals are often served with fresh bread or rice.
